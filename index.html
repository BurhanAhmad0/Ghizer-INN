About Us

Welcome to Ghizer INN Hotel & Restaurant

Crafted in the heart of Ghizer Gahkuch, the headquarters of the district, Ghizer INN offers comfort and convenience. Located on the main Ghizer–Shandoor Road near Sabzi Mandi Gahkuch, we are perfectly positioned for both leisure and business stays.

Enjoy basic amenities including free parking, fast Wi-Fi, easy access to the market, breakfast included, balconies, restrooms, and 24/7 hot water. Whether you’re visiting for work, relaxation, or adventure, we make your stay memorable.

  • Free high-speed Wi-Fi
